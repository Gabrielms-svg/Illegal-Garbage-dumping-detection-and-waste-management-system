About Our Illegal Garbage Dumping Detection System

Overview

Our system uses AI-powered CCTV camera monitoring combined with modern smart waste management tools to detect and prevent illegal garbage dumping in real-time. This technology helps keep communities clean, safe, and environmentally friendly.

The Problem

Illegal garbage dumping creates serious environmental and health challenges:

  • Pollution of streets, water bodies, and natural areas
  • Unpleasant smell and spread of diseases
  • Increased cleaning costs for municipalities
  • Overflowing bins and unmanaged waste
  • Negative impact on community safety and aesthetics

Our Solution

This system provides an intelligent and automated method of detecting and managing waste.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Detection: CCTV cameras with AI identify illegal dumping instantly.
  • Automatic Alerts: Authorities receive immediate notifications with evidence.
  • Smart Bin Monitoring: Sensors track bin capacity and prevent overflow.
  • Data Analytics Dashboard: Shows dumping hotspots and waste generation trends.

Mission

Our mission is to create cleaner and smarter cities through technology. By reducing illegal dumping and improving waste-management efficiency, we move toward a more sustainable future.

Who Benefits

  • Municipal corporations
  • Smart city planners
  • Residential communities
  • Waste-management companies
  • Environmental organizations
